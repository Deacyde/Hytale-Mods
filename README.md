# Hytale Mods

Hytale mods by Deacyde.

## Mods

### GunTurretMod
A deployable gun turret item. Throw it — it lands and deploys an AoE damage zone that deals 8 damage every 0.5 seconds to nearby enemies within an 8-block radius for 30 seconds.

**Install:** Drop `GunTurret-1.0.0.zip` into your Hytale `UserData/Mods/` folder.
